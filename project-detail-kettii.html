KETTII : A game for board-game lovers

About Kettii

A game that builds on top of the popular Ludo game. The game can be played by 2 to 4 players and was intended to be enjoyed in any setting - outdoors or indoors.
Key notes on this game:

  • Can be played by 2 to 4 players.
  • Each player has 3 pieces that are moved after rolling 2 die.
  • Players move around 3 rims until all their 3 pieces reach their home.
  • Players send other players 1 rim back if displaced from a tile.
  • The first player to have all their pieces reach home wins.

A game designed to be enjoyed across all continents.
